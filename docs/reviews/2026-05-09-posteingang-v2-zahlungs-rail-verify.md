## 1. Verdict + summary

**REVISE** — the legal/standard substrate is solid, the wow is real, and the domain-doc has done the hard work locking Wortlaut, Säumnis-norms, Citation-Match-Gate, and Phishing-Gate. But two structural problems veto a direct PROCEED: (a) **the seed data does not contain the IBAN/Empfänger/BIC strings the Citation-Match-Gate must match against** — Gate #8.1 would fail today on 6/6 letters because `body_de` says "auf das untenstehende Konto" with no Konto printed; (b) the cross-feature interaction with Idea-8 (Aussetzung der Vollziehung) is unhandled — a citizen who has filed Einspruch + Aussetzungs-Antrag must NOT see "Zahlung vorbereiten" + "1 % Säumnis nach § 240 AO" parked under the same letter, that is anti-citizen and self-contradicting. Three further attack vectors carry SOFT-REVISE flags. None are unfixable; all must be locked before product-architect spins up.

## 2. Attack-vector-by-attack-vector

### AV1. Speculative-2027 framing honesty — PASS (with one tightening)
The `[Speculative 2027] FITKO-XBezahldienste-Adapter-Hypothese auf Basis IT-Planungsrats-Beschluss 2023/51 + BundID-Postfach`-Frame is defensible. XBezahldienste pflicht-ab 01.01.2026 is a real, dated, sourced anchor; BundID-Postfach exists since Sept 2023; combining the two in 2027 is plausible-aspirational, not made-up. **However**: domain-doc §1 admits the standard regulates "Anbieter-zu-Bezahldienst innerhalb einer Behörden-Online-Strecke" — i.e. the citizen-inbox-aggregation step is not in scope. The locked Speculative-Banner-Wortlaut §289 of the domain-doc must include the verb **"konvergieren"** explicitly so a recruiter-viewer does not misread "BundID + XBezahldienste" as already-shipped. Suggest replacing "Diese Pay-Rail simuliert eine zukünftige Konvergenz" with **"Diese Pay-Rail simuliert eine spekulative 2027-Konvergenz, die heute (Mai 2026) nicht beschlossen ist"** — explicitness over elegance. Otherwise PASS.

### AV2. Säumnis-Wortlaut — passive Information vs Druck-Pattern — SOFT-REVISE
The locked FA-Wortlaut "Bei nicht fristgerechter Zahlung fällt nach § 240 Abs. 1 AO ein Säumniszuschlag von 1 % je angefangenem Monat … an. Die ersten drei Tage gelten als Schonfrist" is fact-stating, not Klarna-imperative — there is no "jetzt", no "Sie müssen", no countdown of cumulative cost. Compared to actual Behörden-Bescheid-Boilerplate it is in fact *milder* (real Bescheide say verbatim the same thing, often with stronger Vollstreckungs-Hinweis). **But**: positioning matters. If this string appears as a static row inside a card *whose primary CTA is "Zahlung vorbereiten"*, the visual gestalt is "fact + path-to-resolve-fact" — that's nudge geometry whether or not the string is passive. Mitigation: place the Säumnis-string **NOT** in the Pay-CTA-card, but in the existing `<NormTooltip>` already attached to the Frist-Chip per V1-spec §10 Z.1043 (`steuerbescheid.saeumniszuschlag_info`). The CTA-card itself shows only IBAN/Betrag/VZ + "Zahlung vorbereiten" + "[Speculative]"-Banner. The Säumnis-fact lives one tap away in the tooltip, not next to the button. SOFT-REVISE: spec must explicitly forbid the Säumnis-string inside the PaymentSheet-modal or PaymentCard.

### AV3. Phishing-Gate UX-Friction (17/18 briefpost) — SOFT-REVISE
Domain-doc §9 makes the Confirmation-Step ("Ich habe die IBAN im Brief mit der hier angezeigten verglichen") mandatory for `auth_channel === 'briefpost'`. Of the V1.5 corpus 17/18 are briefpost; effectively the gate fires on every demo Pay-flow. **This is a feature, not a bug** — it lets the Loom show "App nimmt Bürger:innen-Trust ernst, strukturell" — the same demo lever V1's Citation-Match-Gate uses. PASS *if and only if* the gate is one click (single checkbox + "Weiter"-button), not a full modal-with-prose. Risk: domain-doc §273 verbatim is a 4-sentence Disclaimer block; if rendered as a modal-blocker it costs 5+ seconds of the citizen's time on every Pay-attempt — which kills the 30-second-wow CLAUDE.md demands. SOFT-REVISE: spec must lock the Phishing-Gate as **inline-checkbox inside the PaymentSheet** (not a separate prior modal) with the 4-sentence text rendered as collapsible `<details>`, default-collapsed, with the 1-sentence summary "Behörden-Bescheid in Papierform vor Ihnen — IBAN gegenpr​üfen" visible. One click to expand, one click to confirm. Total friction: 2 clicks, ≤ 3 seconds. Else fails 30-second-wow.

### AV4. "Zahlung vorbereiten" vs "Bezahlen" verb choice — PASS
Domain-doc §283 locks "Zahlung vorbereiten" — and that is correct. The verb "Bezahlen" implies the App initiates the payment; in fact the App generates only an EPC-QR + Clipboard-tokens, the citizen completes the payment in their bank-app. "Bezahlen" is misrepresentation; "Zahlung vorbereiten" is honest. GOV.UK-Pay says "Pay" because GOV.UK Pay actually *takes* the payment (Stripe/Worldpay-backend) — we don't, so we cannot. Honest labelling beats demo-punch here. PASS.

### AV5. Out-of-Scope coherence with Idea 8 (Aussetzung) — HARD-REVISE
This is the single biggest gap. Idea 8 (`aussetzung_vollziehung`-Skelett-Template) and Pay-Rail target the **same** letter archetype `steuerbescheid` and the **same** sticky-action surface. If a citizen has clicked "Aussetzung beantragen" and sent the simulated Antrag, the App still has every reason to render the "Zahlung vorbereiten"-CTA and the § 240 AO-Säumnis-string under the same Brief — because Pay-Rail-state and Aussetzungs-state are not connected in either upstream doc. That is **anti-citizen**: a citizen who is contesting the Bescheid is being prompted to pay it. Mehmet-FA-2024 is exactly the persona where this collides — he has both `frist.typ: 'zahlung'` and `frist.typ: 'einspruch'` rows. Domain-doc §10 #6 explicitly hand-waves Ratenzahlung+Aussetzung as "out of pay-rail", but never specifies that the Pay-CTA must hide or downgrade when an Aussetzungs-Antrag is recorded. **Required fix before PROCEED**: domain-expert must lock the rule "If `letter.replies` contains a Reply with `template === 'aussetzung_vollziehung'` AND the Reply is not yet rejected by the Behörde (always-true in mock), the Pay-CTA renders in **secondary** style with Wortlaut **'Zahlung trotz Aussetzungs-Antrag vorbereiten'** + Disclaimer **'Sie haben am [Datum] Aussetzung der Vollziehung nach § 361 AO beantragt. Eine Zahlung vor der Entscheidung ist möglich, aber nicht erforderlich.'**". This must be a Wortlaut-lock from domain-expert, not a UX call from product-architect. HARD-REVISE.

### AV6. Citation-Match-Strenge vs Demo-Wow — HARD-REVISE
Domain-doc §8 #1–#3 specifies a 3-part match (IBAN-Substring + Betrag-Kontext-Anker + VZ-Verbatim-aus-aktenzeichen). I tested this against the actual seed data: **every IBAN in domain-doc §294's locked Mock-IBAN-Tabelle is not present in the corresponding `body_de` of any of the 6 target letters today.** `letter-mehmet-fa-steuerbescheid-2024` ends with "Bitte zahlen Sie bis zum 12.06.2026 auf das untenstehende Konto." and then has zero further bank-block — no IBAN, no BIC, no Empfänger-line. Same for Schmidt-FA, BGW, IHK, both Beitragsservice-Mahnungen, TK, AOK-freiwillig. The Citation-Match-Gate as locked would trigger the Hand-off-Wortlaut **on 6 of 6 Pay-Rail-target-letters** today. That is REJECT-territory for the demo-walkthrough. Two paths to fix: (a) mock-backend-coder rewrites `body_de` of the 6 letters to append a real Bankverbindung-block ("IBAN: [MOCK] DE10 …  BIC: COLSDE33XXX  Empfänger: Finanzamt Köln-Mitte") and the test `tests/unit/letter-payment-iban.test.ts` validates `body_de` *contains* the IBAN literally. (b) shortcut: spec invents a parallel `letter.bankverbindung`-property and the Citation-Match-Gate is auto-pass when this property is present; this **bypasses** the gate's whole point (the gate is supposed to catch hallucinated IBANs, not be auto-skipped). Path (a) is the only honest one. HARD-REVISE: spec must lock that the seed-rewrite of all 6 (plus optional 7th OWi) `body_de`-strings precedes any UI work; Citation-Match-Gate is a real string-search against `body_de`, not a `letter.bankverbindung`-shortcut.

### AV7. Coherence: pay is not the third step of every Brief — PASS
Of 18 letters: 3 carry `frist.typ: 'zahlung'` (Schmidt-FA, Mehmet-FA, Mehmet-BGW); 4 more (Beitragsservice ×2, IHK, TK, AOK-freiwillig) carry implicit Zahlungs-Erwartung but no `zahlung`-Frist row; 11 are purely informativ (Bewilligungs-Bescheide, Bestätigungen, Termin-Vorschläge). Pay-Rail will be relevant for at most 7 letters. **But** that's enough for the demo arc: of Mehmet's 5 letters, 3 carry payment intent — that's a 60 % surface on the Selbstständige-persona's inbox. The "we close read-reply-pay loop" pitch is a slight overclaim because pay is only step-3 for ~40 % of letters; but as a *loop-completion* over the existing `Letter`-corpus' specifically-payment-bearing archetypes, it is honest. **Required**: research-scout must rewrite the loop-claim as **"we close the read-reply-pay loop for payment-bearing Bescheid-archetypes (40 % of corpus, 100 % of high-friction archetypes)"** to avoid framing-overclaim. PASS with this rewrite.

### AV8. 2027-temporal-relevance of SEPA-out-of-citizen-bank — SOFT-REVISE
Sharp question. In a 2027-Vollausbau-Welt with BundID + EUDI Wallet + state-side direct-debit-mandates stored once, would Behörden ever ask a citizen for a one-shot SEPA-Überweisung from their personal bank? Probably less so — Lastschrift-Mandate-once-and-done is the rational endgame. **But**: (a) Bußgeld and one-off Steuer-Nachzahlung will *always* be one-shot debits because amount is unknown ex-ante and per-Bescheid; (b) recurring Lastschrift requires Mandat-Erteilung which is itself a process the App could orchestrate (out-of-scope by domain-doc §10 #3 — correctly so). Pay-Rail is therefore a 2025-2027-bridge feature for one-shot debits, with declining 2030+ relevance. The Speculative-Banner should reflect that honestly. SOFT-REVISE: research-scout adds a one-line "Pay-Rail addresses one-shot Bescheid-Zahlungen; recurring obligations would in 2030+ migrate to BundID-stored SEPA-Lastschrift-Mandate (out of V2 scope)" to the gap-analysis to forestall a recruiter-counter-question.

### AV9. Empty-state for non-payment letters (12-15 of 18) — PASS
Domain-doc §10 #1 implicitly handles this by gating the CTA on `letter.payment !== undefined`. For letters without payment data (Bewilligungs-, Bestätigungs-, Termin-Briefe) the Pay-CTA is simply not rendered — same affordance-pattern as V1's "Frist im Kalender"-CTA hiding when `fristen.length === 0`. The StickyFristAction component already follows this pattern (`{earliest && earliest.citation_match !== false && ...}`). PASS — provided product-architect re-uses the same conditional-render pattern, not a grayed-out-disabled-button pattern. Spec must explicitly forbid disabled-Pay-Button in empty-state.

### AV10. A11y / cognitive load — 5 CTAs in StickyFristAction — SOFT-REVISE
Today's StickyFristAction renders: Frist-Chip + (optional Kalender-Button) + (optional View-Sent-Button) + Reply-Button + Overflow-Menu (3 items). With Pay-Rail the layout becomes: Frist-Chip + Kalender + Pay + Reply + View-Sent + Overflow. Six visible affordances on a payment-bearing Bescheid. That is over the gov.uk-minimalism budget — gov.uk-Pay at most renders 2 primary CTAs in any service. The 5-second EPC-QR-wow (research-scout's framing) is fine **inside** the PaymentSheet-modal; it must not bleed into the Sticky-Band. SOFT-REVISE: spec must lock CTA-priority — primary is **"Zahlung vorbereiten"** *only when* `frist.typ === 'zahlung'` is the earliest unfulfilled Frist; Reply is demoted to secondary; Kalender + View-Sent move into Overflow-Menu. If the earliest Frist is `widerspruch`/`nachweis`/`antragstellung`, Reply stays primary and Pay drops to secondary. Visual-priority follows Frist-typ, not feature-arrival-order. Without this, the Sticky-Band becomes a CTA-supermarket and the citizen-respectful register is lost.

### AV11. Citation-Match-Rule machine-spec lock — HARD-REVISE
Domain-doc §8 specifies a regex (`[A-Z]{2}\d{2}[A-Z0-9 ]{16,32}`) for IBAN, but does **not** lock: (i) whether Substring-match is whitespace-tolerant (real Behörden-Bescheide vary: "DE12 2005 …" vs "DE122005…"); (ii) whether the Betrag-context-anchor accepts thousands-separator variants (1.247,00 € vs 1247,00 € vs 1.247 €); (iii) whether VZ-match is exact-equal or normalized (e.g. "[MOCK] 22/345/12345" vs "22/345/12345" — Mock-prefix in `letter.aktenzeichen`-field but absent in body-Aktenzeichen-Zeile). These are not nits — they decide whether Mehmet-FA's gate fires GREEN or HAND-OFF. Without lock, product-architect will invent the rule, and the rule will silently bend toward whatever makes the demo-letters pass. HARD-REVISE: domain-expert must lock the three normalization-rules verbatim before product-architect speccs Pay-Rail.

### AV12. Mock-IBAN realism + count discrepancy — HARD-REVISE
The locked Mock-IBAN-Tabelle in domain-doc §294 has **8 rows** (not 6 as research-scout proposed): Mehmet-FA, Schmidt-FA, Schmidt-TK, Mehmet-AOK, Schmidt-Beitragsservice, Mehmet-Beitragsservice, Mehmet-IHK, Mehmet-BGW. Plus a 9th conditional (Berliner Sparkasse for OWi if a Bußgeld-Letter is seeded). Research-scout's "6 Archetypen" framing is wrong — the actual count of payment-bearing letters in V1.5 corpus is **7 deterministic + 1 optional**. The discrepancy is not load-bearing for the verdict but the spec must reconcile the count. More worryingly: domain-doc §297 instructs mock-backend-coder to **"vor dem Schreiben in `letters.json` muss jede IBAN per `IBAN.electronicFormat()` + mod-97-10 lokal validiert werden"** — but the doc itself does not show the validation result. I have no way to verify checksums by inspection. HARD-REVISE: domain-expert (or mock-backend-coder if delegated) must run the IBAN-checksum pre-flight and embed the result in the locked-Tabelle as `checksum_verified: true` per row, **before** seed-rewrite. Without this, we may ship 8 letters with a 1-in-100 chance any single IBAN fails mod-97-10 silently — which a real bank-app scanner would refuse to parse, killing the EPC-QR-demo on stage.

## 3. New gaps you found (upstream-missed)

1. **`body_de` does not contain IBAN-strings today** — most consequential gap. Six target letters' body-text ends with "auf das untenstehende Konto" and then no Konto-block. Citation-Match-Gate fails 6/6. Mock-backend-coder must extend each `body_de` with a real Bankverbindung-Footer-block (3 lines: IBAN / BIC / Empfänger) before any UI work. Domain-doc §297 hints at this but does not lock it as a precondition.

2. **Aussetzung × Pay-CTA cross-state is unspecified** (see AV5). Domain-doc §10 says Aussetzung is out-of-pay-rail-scope — but Aussetzung *changes the meaning* of the Pay-CTA on the same letter. Cross-feature wordings must be domain-locked, not architect-discretion.

3. **`letter-aok-rechnung-zuzahlung` and `letter-mehmet-beitragsservice-mahnung` carry no `frist.typ: 'zahlung'`** but functionally demand payment. Either spec adds payment-frist rows to seed (with `citation_match: true` against the body's "Bitte zahlen Sie"-string) or Pay-CTA condition must become `letter.payment !== undefined OR archetype ∈ {beitragsservice-mahnung, krankenkasse-beitrag, ihk-beitrag, berufsgenossenschaft-beitrag}` even without explicit Zahlungs-Frist. Domain-expert must rule which.

4. **Mehmet-FA-2024 has `frist.typ: 'zahlung'` `citation_match: true` AND `frist.typ: 'einspruch'` `citation_match: false`**. The StickyFristAction's `earliest = fristen[0]` picks one Frist; today it's whichever sorts first. With Pay-Rail this becomes ambiguous — does the band say "Frist Zahlung 12.06" or "Frist Einspruch 12.06"? Both share the same datum. Spec must lock how the band labels Frist when two Fristen on the same letter share a datum.

5. **Datenschutz-Cockpit-Toggle for Pay-Rail (`Art. 6 Abs. 1 lit. a` per domain-doc §7)** — no V1 Cockpit-toggle UI exists today. Domain-doc presumes it; product-architect would have to spec a new Cockpit-toggle surface for one feature. Either Pay-Rail uses **lit. b (Vertragserfüllung)** like the rest of the App's core-feature surface (Reply uses lit. b implicitly) — simpler, no new Cockpit-toggle — OR Cockpit-toggle work is part of Pay-Rail-V2 scope, which doubles the build. Domain-expert recommended lit. a + Toggle without costing the toggle. Concept-verifier preference: lit. b, no new Toggle, no new Cockpit-surface — pay is part of the App's core promised function, citizens consent at onboarding. Domain-expert should reconsider.

## 5. Risks accepted-but-named (for spec § "Known limitations")

- **Säumnis-Wortlaut is fact-stating but visually adjacent to Pay-CTA** — mitigated by AV2 placement-rule, but the visual-adjacency is hard to fully eliminate. Document it as an accepted residual.
- **EPC-QR is one-way: App → Bank-App** — no confirmation that the citizen actually paid; the App never sees the Bank-App's response. Activity-Log entry is "initiated", not "completed". Domain-doc §10 #10 already covers this; spec should mirror verbatim.
- **`auth_channel: 'briefpost'` on 17/18 letters means Confirmation-Step fires on every demo Pay-flow** — this is a feature for the trust-narrative but adds 2-3 seconds to every Loom Pay-cut. Risk-accept with the inline-checkbox mitigation.
- **No real Bank-App-Deeplink** — domain-doc §3 correctly forbids it; the dekoratives "Banking-App öffnen"-button is optional and may be cut for simplicity. Recommend cutting it.
- **Mock-IBANs use real BLZ-Ranges** for plausibility; if a Loom-viewer types one into their real bank-app the bank-app will refuse (Konto-Nr. invalid). Document in `[MOCK]`-Banner that "IBANs sind synthetisch, sie repräsentieren keine echten Konten".

## 6. Recommended scope-cut for V2 shippability

Defer to V2.1 (or merge into a later wave):

- **Sub-tertiary "Banking-App öffnen"-deeplink-Mock** (domain-doc §3 #3) — cut entirely. Adds Mock-Theater without functional value beyond what the EPC-QR + Clipboard-Copy already provide. If recruiter asks "warum kein Bank-App-Deeplink?" the answer "öffentliches Schema existiert nicht, EPC-QR ist EU-Standard und in jeder DACH-Bank-App scanbar" is stronger than a fake-deeplink.
- **Behörden-IBAN-Whitelist** (domain-doc §8 #4) — interesting Phishing-defense but doubles the V2-data-modeling work. Defer to V2.1 when Idea 6 (Authentizitäts-Proof) is also being built; together they make a coherent "Phishing-Defense-Welle". V2 ships with Citation-Match-Gate + Phishing-Confirmation-Step only.
- **`payment_initiated`-Activity-Log-Eintrag persistent in Datenschutz-Cockpit** — V2 logs to `localStorage` as planned; the Cockpit-surface that displays per-letter Activity-Log is already V1, no new Cockpit-UI work. **Do not** add a Cockpit-toggle for Pay-Rail (per gap #5) — keep the surface flat.

## 7. Recommendation to product-architect (if PROCEED after revisions)

Spec a **`<PaymentSheet>`** modal-component opened from a third primary CTA in `StickyFristAction` ("Zahlung vorbereiten") that is rendered conditionally only when `letter.payment !== undefined` AND `earliest-unfulfilled-Frist.typ === 'zahlung'` AND no successful `aussetzungs_vollziehung`-Reply exists. The Sheet contains: (1) `[Speculative 2027]`-Header banner verbatim from domain-doc §289 (with the "die heute (Mai 2026) nicht beschlossen ist"-tightening); (2) inline-Phishing-Confirmation-Checkbox with collapsible 4-sentence-Disclaimer (default-collapsed, 1-sentence-summary visible); (3) three read-only Kopier-Felder IBAN/Betrag/Verwendungszweck with per-field Copy-Button + Citation-Match-Indicator (✓ when matched in `body_de`, hand-off-row when failed); (4) full-width EPC-QR rendered client-side from EPC069-12 v3.1 with Version `002` UTF-8; (5) DSGVO-Footer-Banner verbatim from domain-doc §269 with Rechtsgrundlage **Art. 6 Abs. 1 lit. b DSGVO Vertragserfüllung** (NOT lit. a per gap #5). Activity-Log entry `payment_initiated` written on EPC-QR-render OR first-Copy-Click. Säumnis-string lives only in the existing `<NormTooltip>` on the Frist-Chip outside the Sheet. Aussetzungs-Antrag-state changes the Pay-CTA-label per AV5-locked-Wortlaut and downgrades it to secondary visual-style. Seed-rewrite of all payment-target `body_de`-strings is a hard precondition with a green `tests/unit/letter-payment-iban.test.ts` before UI-work. CTA-priority in `StickyFristAction` is recomputed per Frist-typ-of-earliest-unfulfilled, not per feature-arrival-order — Kalender + View-Sent move to Overflow on Pay-relevant letters. No Bank-App-Deeplink, no Cockpit-toggle, no Behörden-IBAN-Whitelist in V2.
